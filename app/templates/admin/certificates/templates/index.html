Managing Certificate Templates

Certificate templates allow you to create different styles of certificates that can be assigned to specific courses.

The default template is used for courses that don't have a specific template assigned.

Tips:

  • Use the Preview button to see what your certificate design will look like
  • You can set any template as the default template
  • Assign templates to individual courses in the course settings
